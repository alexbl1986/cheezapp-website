Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters. Cheez is designed with privacy at its core. All photo processing happens on your device, and your photos never leave your phone unless you choose to share them.

This Privacy Policy describes how Cheez ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and authentication credentials (password or third-party auth tokens for Google/Apple Sign-In).
  • User Settings: We store your preferences including quality threshold settings and tutorial completion status.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us at support@cheezapp.top, we collect your email address and message content.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Data: The App accesses your device camera to capture photos and perform real-time face detection. All processing happens on your device using Apple's Vision framework.
  • Photo Storage: Selfies you capture are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers.
  • Face Detection Data: The App analyzes facial landmarks (19+ detection points) to provide quality scoring and guidance. This analysis happens on-device in real-time and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous analytics data including app launches, feature usage, tutorial completion, and crash reports to improve the App.
  • Device Information: We collect device type, iOS version, and app version for compatibility and support purposes.

1.3 Subscription and Payment Information

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple provides us with:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, cancelled)
  • Subscription type (weekly or yearly)
  • Purchase and renewal dates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Features: Camera access and face detection enable real-time selfie guidance and auto-capture functionality.
  • Quality Analysis: Facial landmark detection and image analysis provide quality scores and improvement tracking.
  • User Accounts: Authentication services (Firebase Auth) enable cloud sync of your settings and preferences across devices.
  • App Improvement: Analytics help us understand feature usage and identify bugs or performance issues.
  • Customer Support: We use your email to respond to support requests and provide assistance.
  • Subscription Management: We verify subscription status to provide premium features to paying users.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All photos you capture with Cheez are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to cloud servers.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you create an account, we store the following in Firebase (Google Cloud):

  • Account information (email, name)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Tutorial completion status
  • Personal best scores (metadata only, not photos)

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Auth
  • No storage of payment information
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

Purpose: Authentication, cloud storage of settings, and analytics

Data Shared: Email, name, app usage patterns, device information

Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

Purpose: Serving rewarded video ads to unlock additional selfies

Data Shared: Device advertising ID, IP address, app usage data

Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads

4.3 Apple Vision Framework

Purpose: On-device face detection and image analysis

Data Processing: All processing happens on your device. No data is sent to Apple or our servers.

4.4 RevenueCat (if applicable)

Purpose: Subscription management and analytics

Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, subscription status, purchase events

Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase and AdMob as described above, bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Important: Your photos are never shared with us or third parties unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device's share functionality.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data by emailing support@cheezapp.top. We will provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format within 30 days.

6.2 Correction

You can update your account information within the App's Settings screen or by contacting us.

6.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data:

  • In-app: Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: support@cheezapp.top with subject "Delete My Account"

Upon deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information
  • Cloud-synced settings
  • Usage analytics tied to your account

Note: Photos stored locally on your device must be deleted manually through your device's Photos app or by uninstalling Cheez.

6.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can limit analytics collection through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Share iPhone Analytics (disable).

6.5 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (disable).

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account, then removed within 30 days
  • Photos: Stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete them
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Emails: Retained for 1 year for customer service purposes

8. Children's Privacy

Cheez is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us at support@cheezapp.top.

9. International Users

Cheez is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK: We comply with GDPR requirements. Your data rights under GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection) are honored. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, email support@cheezapp.top with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • We will notify you through the App or via email for material changes
  • Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@cheezapp.top
Website: cheezapp.top

Summary: We respect your privacy. Your photos stay on your device. We only collect what's necessary to provide a great selfie experience. You have full control over your data.
